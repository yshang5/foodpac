Our Story

About foodPac

We're on a mission to give every independent restaurant access to professional, eco-friendly branded packaging — at a price that makes sense.

Why We Started foodPac

Over 90% of independent restaurants in North America use generic, unbranded packaging — not because they don't care about their brand, but because custom packaging has historically been too expensive and complicated to access.

Professional packaging design alone can cost $500–$2,000 per project. Minimum order quantities force restaurants to over-purchase, tying up cash and creating waste. And eco-friendly options come at a premium most small businesses can't afford.

We built foodPac to change that. By combining AI-powered design with smart supply chain aggregation, we make it possible for any restaurant — regardless of size or budget — to have beautiful, branded, sustainable packaging.
